Accidental & Emergency Care

At Harmony Health Care Center, we are dedicated to providing immediate and expert medical assistance for all emergencies and accidents, round the clock. Our 24-hour emergency unit and ambulance service are staffed with highly trained emergency management teams, prepared to deliver exceptional care and treatment swiftly and efficiently to patients in urgent need. Our state-of-the-art Harmony Ambulance fleet is equipped with advanced patient monitoring systems, ensuring that critical patients receive continuous care from the moment of contact. These systems allow our first responders to transmit crucial data, such as ECG readings, directly to our specialized Harmony Heart Centre.

BLood Bank

Established in 1996, the Harmony Blood Bank was one the first blood banks established in private sector medical care in Sri Lanka. It works closely with the National Blood Transfusion Center and provides the required supply of blood for our patients. The blood bank’s stringent quality standards and strict adherence to screening donors ensures that the highest quality blood products are readily available for the individual needs of patients. Its dedicated staff come under the purview of 4 doctors and are highly trained to handle all emergency procedures.
